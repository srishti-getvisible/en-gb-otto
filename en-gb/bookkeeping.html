Leave Manual Bookkeeping Behind

Tired of downloading bank statements, sorting through receipts, and manually tagging every transaction? Otto AI-driven bookkeeping software takes care of the heavy lifting, so you can focus on the work that brings in income.

Otto AI bookkeeping software keeps your records tidy, your documents in order, and your stress levels low, no last-minute scrambling required. Staying organised and compliant has never been simpler.

  • Bank-grade security with SOC 2 controls underway

  • Supports multiple business entities with clear, transparent pricing

Clear Financial Insights, Whenever You Need Them

Spreadsheets quickly go out of date, and they won’t help you make confident decisions. Otto AI-driven bookkeeping gives you up-to-date visibility across your finances, so you can price your services accurately, plan for tax bills, and pay yourself with confidence.

  • Real-time dashboards and key metrics tailored to service-led businesses

  • Daily account reconciliation using dual data feeds (Plaid + QuickBooks)

  • Seamless revenue matching across platforms

Real Support, Not Just Software

With Our AI bookkeeping software, you get smart automation when it suits you, and real people when it matters. Your accounts aren’t just auto-generated, they’re checked and reviewed by qualified experts, blending the speed of AI with the reassurance of human oversight.

FAQs

How does AI bookkeeping software save time for entrepreneurs?



AI bookkeeping tools like Otto AI automatically pull data from your bank feeds, sort your transactions, and update your cash flow in real time. This removes the need for hours of manual data entry, freeing you up to focus on your work and running your business.

Can I track my cash flow in real time?



Yes. Our AI bookkeeping software provides a live dashboard that updates your profit & loss, balance sheet, and cash flow metrics as transactions come in. This gives you instant visibility over your finances, helping you make confident, timely decisions.

How does Otto AI Bookkeeping categorise my transactions automatically?



Otto AI bookkeeping software uses machine learning to sort most transactions within 30 minutes of syncing with your account. Anything unusual is reviewed by our finance team to ensure your reports stay accurate and relevant.

Will my accounts be ready for tax season?



Absolutely. Otto AI bookkeeping software organises your transactions throughout the year with tax preparation in mind. Your records stay clean, up to date, and audit-ready, so you’re never caught off guard at year-end.

Is my financial data secure with Otto AI?



Yes. Otto AI bookkeeping software uses bank-grade encryption, strict permissions, and regular third-party audits to keep your financial data safe. You can manage your finances with complete peace of mind.

How does Otto AI bookkeeping connect to my bank accounts?



Otto AI bookkeeping software links securely to your accounts using trusted services like Plaid. This enables live transaction feeds and automatic receipt matching, no manual uploading required.

Can I get support from real financial experts?



Definitely. While Otto AI handles the automation, you also have access to real accountants and finance professionals via live chat, email, or phone, so you’re never left on your own.

Is the dashboard easy to use if I’m not an accountant?



Very. The interface is designed for non-finance users, with colour-coded insights, intuitive categories, and plain-language explanations, perfect for creative professionals and business owners.

Will Otto AI work with my current tools and reduce app overload?



Yes. Otto AI bookkeeping software connects with QuickBooks, your bank, payment processors, invoicing tools, and tax software, all in one dashboard. It brings your financial systems together, reducing the need for multiple apps and eliminating manual reconciliations.

How long does it take to get set up?



Most users are up and running in under an hour. Simply link your bank accounts, and Otto takes care of the rest, ideal for those looking for fast, fuss-free bookkeeping.

Is Otto AI a good fit for entrepreneurs and sole traders?



Yes. Otto is purpose-built for business owners, creatives, and sole traders. Whether you’re just getting started or managing multiple clients, Otto AI bookkeeping software helps keep your finances organised and stress-free as your business grows.
