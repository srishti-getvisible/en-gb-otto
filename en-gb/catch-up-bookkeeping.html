Catch Up Bookkeeping FAQs

What is catch-up bookkeeping?



It’s the process of organising and reconciling financial records when you’ve fallen behind bringing everything up to date so you’re accurate, compliant, and ready for tax season.

Who is this service for?



Otto AI catch-up bookkeeping is ideal for self-employed professionals and creative photographers, podcasters, designers, who need to get their finances back on track.

How long does the process take?



Depending on how far behind you are, most clean-ups are completed in days not weeks thanks to our AI-powered systems and expert support.

How do I catch up on months of bookkeeping quickly?



Otto AI uses automation to clean and organise backlogged records fast turning chaos into clean, tax-ready books.

What happens after the catch-up is complete?



You can continue with Otto AI monthly bookkeeping services to ensure your records stay accurate and up to date.

Can you handle years of unorganised receipts?



Yes, secure uploads and AI-powered sorting let us process even the messiest record piles and turn them into clean accounts.

Is there expert review involved?



Definitely. Every transaction Otto AI processes is double-checked by our accounting team for accuracy and reliability.

Is my data secure during the process?



Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption and secure handling to protect all sensitive data throughout the process.

Will my books be tax-ready once complete?



Yes. You'll receive categorised, expert-reviewed records and reports that are ready for filing.

What kind of reports will I receive?



You’ll get clear reports on your income, expenses, and cash flow, so you can make confident financial decisions without needing accounting experience.
