How does AI-powered bookkeeping save small businesses time?



Otto AI automatically categorises new transactions from your bank or accounting software, with human accountants verifying accuracy. UK small businesses typically save over 5 hours each week.

What makes Otto AI different from traditional accounting software?



Otto uniquely blends AI automation with expert UK accountants, combining bookkeeping, invoicing, Self Assessment, contracts, and company setup into a single, easy-to-use platform. It offers a free tier for businesses processing up to £20K annually.

Can I easily manage invoices and payments?



Absolutely—manage deposits, milestones, recurring bills, and multi-currency invoices effortlessly. Otto AI sends smart reminders helping you get paid up to 30% faster, syncing payments instantly to your accounts.

How does Otto AI simplify tax preparation?



Otto AI tracks allowable expenses, securely stores receipts, and provides quarterly accountant reviews. It ensures your books are HMRC-ready and handles electronic submissions of any required tax documents.

Is my financial data secure with Otto AI?



Otto AI protects your data using bank-level encryption, strict access controls, continuous monitoring, and regular security audits, including SOC-2 compliance processes underway.

Can Otto AI separate personal and business finances?



Yes—Otto AI clearly tags mixed expenses for instant compliant bookkeeping. If needed, Otto AI also assists with registering as a sole trader or forming a limited company.

How does the real-time dashboard benefit my business finances?



Within an hour of connecting accounts, Otto’s dashboard provides live updates on cash flow, profit & loss, and balance sheets, giving immediate clarity on your financial status.

Does Otto AI offer support from real UK finance experts?



Yes—all plans include responsive UK-based chat support, with premium options providing dedicated accountants and expert calls, typically answered within an hour.

What legal contract support does Otto AI provide?



Otto AI offers over 150 UK-specific legal templates, an AI assistant for drafting and summarising contracts, integrated e-signature functionality, and automatic linking of contracts to invoices.
