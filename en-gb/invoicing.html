Invoicing FAQs

Why use Otto invoicing software?



Otto AI invoicing tool is smart, easy to use, and completely free. It offers customisable templates and helpful automations, making it ideal for creatives and small businesses who want to stay organised without spending extra.

Can I track whether an invoice has been paid?



Yes. Our Invoicing Software allows you to monitor the status of every invoice and automatically sends polite reminders, so you’ll always know what’s been paid, what’s outstanding, and what might need chasing.

What payment methods are supported?



Otto AI Billing Software supports a range of payment options including bank transfers, debit/credit cards, PayPal, and more. You can enable your preferred options and Otto AI Invoicing adds one-click payment buttons to each invoice.

Does the invoicing software calculate totals and VAT automatically?



It does. Otto AI auto-calculates totals, VAT, discounts, and other figures in real-time, saving you time and reducing the risk of errors.

What should be included in an invoice?



A proper invoice should list the invoice number, date, your business details, a short description of the services provided, payment terms, due date, and accepted payment methods. Our free invoicing software helps ensure you include everything that’s required.

Should I include VAT on my invoices?



If you're VAT-registered or required to charge VAT, you should always include it on your invoices. Otto AI makes this easy by handling VAT calculations automatically to help you stay compliant.

Can I save client details for future use?



Absolutely. Otto AI Billing software securely stores your client information so future invoices take just seconds to generate, just select the client and the rest is filled in for you.

Can I customize my invoices?



Yes. You can fully personalise your invoices by adding your logo, branding, preferred currency, VAT rate, and more, ensuring every invoice feels professional and on-brand.

What if I just need to create a one-off invoice quickly?



No problem. If you need to create a single invoice without signing up or using the full platform, try our free invoice software . It’s perfect for freelancers who need something quick, just enter your details and download the invoice instantly.

Can I send and track invoices from my phone?



Yes! Otto AI works seamlessly on mobile, so you can create, send, and track invoices wherever you are.
